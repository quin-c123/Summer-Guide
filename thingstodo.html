Things to do

Little Island

When: Anytime Where: 14th St, 10th Avenue Cost: free

If you're looking for a beautiful and fun place to go this summer, Little Island is the place to go!
Stroll through the gardens or watch the sunset on the hudson river. Or, bring your kids and try the dance pads and see how long you can hula-hoop!

Riverbank State Park (Pool)

When: Anytime Where: 145th St, Riverside Drive Cost: $2 /adult, $1 /child

Wanna go swimming? Just visit Riverank State Park. There, you'll find a pool, some playgrounds, and more.
At just $2 per person, this is sure to deliver a fun time!

Bow Bridge at Central Park

When: Anytime Where: 73 St, Central Park Cost: free

This bridge, dubbed "the bow bridge", is Central Park's longest bridge.
It's one of the most iconic and photographed features of Central Park.
Built in 1862, this Victorian-era bridge spans 60 feet across the Central Park Lake and connects Cherry Hill and the Ramble.

Movie Night at Bryant Park

When: Date not yet known. Where: Bryant Park, Fountain Terrace Cost: free

Enjoy a feature film under the stars on the lawn at the park! Go to Bryant Park around 5:00 for this iconic New York summer activity.
